Application Informtion:

The purpose of this application is to allow a user via their mobile device to alert a predefined set of contacts with a simple push of a button. The alerts will be sent via SMS and/or email. The receipents cannot communicate or reply directly back to the alerts sent. Every person on the list will receive the same information and can take action from there. The notifications are not sent to any emergency service such as the police or the fire department.
